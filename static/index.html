Meet the Agents

Vigil uses a three-stage multi-agent pipeline. Each agent is a specialized LLM with tailored instructions, running on Azure AI Foundry. They communicate sequentially — the output of one becomes the input of the next — ensuring separation of concerns, auditability, and composability.

01

Indexer & Extractor

Parses documents and extracts structured facts — dates, amounts, parties, obligations — into a JSON fact sheet.

View details
02

Analyzer

Compares, checks compliance, cross-references facts, and identifies conflicts, gaps, and deviations.

View details
03

Advisor

Produces human-readable reports — executive summaries, risk highlights, remediation plans, and action items.

View details
